# Governance & Team Information

**Project Name:** Smart Learning for Independence
**Owner:** Windgap Foundation (LLND & Transition)
**Delivery Site:** LLND Learning Hub, Banksmeadow (Bayside Council)
**Duration:** Initial 6‑month delivery cycle (24 modules), within the overall grant period (to June 2027)
**Participants:** Target 50 (adults and youth with disabilities incl. CALD, women, and learning difficulties)

## Governance & Roles
- **Sponsor/Sign‑off:** CEO/Service Director
- **Project Lead:** LLND Trainer – Inclusive Education
- **Delivery Team:** LLND educators, support workers, SLES leads, IT coordinator
- **Allies:** Families/carers, participants, Transurban volunteers (for co‑facilitation and showcase day)
- **Advisory Input:** Participant co‑design group (quarterly)

## Cadence
- Weekly delivery stand‑up (15 mins)
- Fortnightly educator sync (60 mins)
- Monthly steering update (30 mins)
- Quarterly review (impact + improvements)

## Timeline & Milestones
- **Phase 0:** Prep & Procurement (Weeks 1–4)
- **Phase 1:** Pilot & Baseline (Weeks 5–6)
- **Phase 2:** Core Delivery (Weeks 7–32)
- **Phase 3:** Showcase & Handover (Weeks 33–36)
